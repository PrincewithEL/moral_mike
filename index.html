About Our Project...

Our website, is dedicated to promoting moral awareness and ethical living. Through engaging content such as animations, videos, and images, we provide valuable resources and discussions on topics like right from wrong, substance abuse, and the influence of social media.

Utilising tools like Blender, Photoshop, and Audacity, we aim to offer an interactive and informative experience to help strengthen personal and societal morals.

All images on the website apart from photographs of ourselves and the video we produced above, were sourced from copyright free or attribution required sites including DevianArt, Pexels, Unsplash, FlatIcon, and FreePik.
