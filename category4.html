What YOU Can Do...

You can make a big difference in your community and the world! Start by making good choices and leading by example. Talk about the importance of morals with friends and family. Use social media to share positive messages and stories about doing the right thing, using hashtags like #BuildingAMoralTomorrow.

Get involved in community service projects to help others and make a tangible impact. Remember, every small action counts. By spreading awareness and encouraging others to live morally, you contribute to creating a kinder, more respectful world. Together, we can inspire positive change and build a brighter future.

Resources

Below is a list of books, articles, videos, and courses, to assist you as you embark on your journey towards a moral life:

  • The Moral Compass by William Bennett (Bennett, 1995) - This book offers stories and essays that explore different moral values.
  • Meditations by Marcus Aurelius (Aurelius, M., 2006) - A collection of personal writings by the Roman Emperor Marcus Aurelius, offering guidance on living a virtuous life.
  • Beyond Good and Evil by Friedrich Nietzsche (Nietzsche, F., 2003). - Nietzsche explores the concept of morality and challenges traditional ideas about good and evil.
  • The Moral Landscape by Sam Harris (Harris, S., 2010) - Harris argues for a scientific understanding of morality and explores how science can inform our ethical decisions.
  • The Road to Character by David Brooks (Brooks, D., 2015) - Brooks examines the importance of moral character and virtues such as humility, kindness, and honesty.
  • The River and the Source by Margaret A. Ogola (Ogola, M., 2004) - An epic story spanning cultures, it tells the lives of three generations of women. It traces the story of Akoko in her rich traditional Luo setting, through to the children who live and die in the 20th century.
  • Coursera (Cousera, 2024) and Khan Academy (Khan Academy, 2024) - These platforms offer courses on ethics and moral philosophy that can deepen your understanding.
  • Foundations of Ethics (Odhiambo, 2009) - A Critical Reader in Moral and Social Philosophy aims at stimulating and encouraging the reader to undertake better philosophical thinking about ethical questions.
  • KidsHealth.org (KidsHealth.org., 2024) - Provides advice and articles on making good choices and dealing with peer pressure.
  • TED Talks on Morality and Ethics (TED, 2024) - Inspiring talks that offer new perspectives and ideas on living a moral life.
